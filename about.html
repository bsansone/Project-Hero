About


Mission Satement

Project Hero connects Chicago students with relatable role models in their local community to build relationships and foster academic growth.


What We Do

Project Hero connects Chicago students with relatable role models in their local community to build relationships and foster academic growth. Students and parents are able to meet and connect with these role models in-person at our events, through our newsletter and our Hero Map. We want young students to be inspired by these relatable role models so that they can set their goals high and also use our resources to get through any obstacle in their academic career.


Our Program

Project Hero is based mainly in Chicago and its communities so that students can easily relate to their role models. We noticed that a large percentage of Chicago high school students are not gaining degrees or even pursuing college and we wanted to help. Project Hero is here to make community events where you can come and learn about the role models who are from your neighborhood, ask questions or get help with any school questions such as applying to college, filling out FAFSA, scholarships, etc. and get inspired.


Our History

The idea for Project Hero was the result of research done at the Illinois Institute of Technology, through the course:

397-400: Interprofessional by Design: Big Data Science   the Urban Experience Challenge -- Developing Insights from Large Data Sets that can Inspire Solutions to Human Needs in se Urban Environment.

Our journey to Project Hero was a semester longs worth of research, feedback, and big data that lead us to this community initiative that you see here today.
